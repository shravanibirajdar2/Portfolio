Projects

River Water Cleaning Boat

The River Water Cleaning Boat is an innovative, eco-friendly solution designed to tackle the growing problem of water pollution in rivers. This semi-autonomous boat is engineered to collect floating debris such as plastic waste, leaves, and other pollutants from the water surface, helping to restore the cleanliness and health of aquatic environments.

Water Level Indicator

The Water Level Indicator is a simple yet effective electronic device designed to monitor and display the water level in tanks, reservoirs, or containers. This project helps prevent water overflow or dry run of pumps, promoting efficient water usage and management.
